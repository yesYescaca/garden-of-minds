# Garden of Minds

*A living exploration of intelligence, life, minds, and the many possible ways they may exist in the universe.*

---

## What is the Garden of Minds?

The Garden of Minds is my long-term attempt to explore one of the most fascinating questions I can think of:

**What is intelligence, and how would we recognize it if it looked nothing like ourselves?**

Humans often define intelligence using human standards: language, technology, science, mathematics, art, and culture.

But human intelligence may be only one example among many possible forms.

Could a colony be intelligent?

Could an ecosystem be intelligent?

Could an artificial intelligence be intelligent without being alive?

Could an alien mind think in ways completely unfamiliar to us?

The purpose of this project is not to provide definitive answers, but to explore these questions as deeply and honestly as possible.

---

## What will be found here?

This repository serves as both a research journal and an evolving atlas.

Contents may include:

* Notes and reflections
* Questions and thought experiments
* Explorations of biology, evolution, and cognition
* Hypothetical species and alien minds
* Discussions of artificial intelligence
* Ideas from neuroscience, philosophy, and astrobiology
* Revisions of previous beliefs and conclusions

Many entries are works in progress.

Ideas will change.

Mistakes will be made.

Assumptions will be challenged.

That is part of the process.

---

## Guiding Principle

One of the first ideas explored in this project is:

> Human intelligence is not intelligence itself.
>
> It is one example of intelligence among many possible forms.

The Garden of Minds exists to explore what those other forms might be.
